What We Do

We are a video production company based in Waterloo Region focused on telling stories that make a difference. We approach each project by working with our clients to develop a strong understanding of their purpose and the goals for their video. We use this understanding to best craft a meaningful story.

NON PROFIT

We believe that these organizations have some of the most important stories to tell. Video is the perfect medium to bring their mission to life. We believe in the good work that these organizations do in our communities and strive to align ourselves with values and passion their people dedicate each day to.

STARTUP & SMALL BUSINESS

Whether it’s pitching your concept, celebrating your team, scaling your business, showcasing your product or making a sale, we know how important it is to tell a concise story that embodies your culture and communicates your value.

EVENTS

The true magic of what an organization does can often be found when their community comes together. We believe an event is much more than people networking and eating from the buffet. Each event exists because of dedicated people that believe in the work they do. We want to tell those stories.
